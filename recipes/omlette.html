Omlette

Description

Omlette is a really easy and tasty recipe to make. Who doesn't like eggs, right? Let's get into it!

These are all the ingredients you will need:

  • Eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Bread

Now, follow these steps to cook the perfect omlette:

  1. Turn on the stove, add a few drops of olive oil and let it heat for 1 minute
  2. Crack the eggs and add them into the pan
  3. Scramble the eggs using a spoon, do it slowly
  4. Allow the eggs to cook until they get a nice yellow/goldish tint
  5. Place the eggs on a plate, add salt, proceed to eat with your bread of choice
